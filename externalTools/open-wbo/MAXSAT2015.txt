The MaxSAT solver optiriss shall be executed in four different ways during the MaxSAT competition:

1) in all complete tracks, please use the following command lines:

./optiriss -solverConfig=INCSOLVE <inputfile>
./optiriss -solverConfig=INCSOLVE -algorithm=0 -incremental=1 <inputfile>

2) in all incomplete tracks, please use the following configurations of the tool:

./optiriss -solverConfig=INCSOLVE -algorithm=0 -incremental=1 -incomplete <inputfile>
./optiriss -solverConfig=INCSOLVE -incomplete <inputfile>
